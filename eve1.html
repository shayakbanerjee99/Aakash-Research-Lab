Tekmux 1.0

Held on 11th & 12th August 2017, TEKMUX 1.0 was our very first Tri-Hackathon fest. The events were technical and non-technical, developing apps, websites, electrical projects and pumped up the contestants by encouraging them to think out of the box and spontaneously solve quizzes

Tekmux 2.0

TEKMUX 1.0 laid the foundation of great things that 2018 was destined for and TEKMUX 2.0 followed suit.The event was held on 30th August & 1st September, 2018. Making TEKMUX 2.0 even more professional and thrilling, the Elek-A-Thon was an event that tested each participant's technical skills in a way never done before. Adding a swish of the corporate world, Baggage Battle, Pictothon and Junk Art gave every participant the right rung to climb up the ladder of innovation with a vision

Tekmux 3.0

"The first time might be a fluke, and the second time might be a coincidence. But the third time becomes a tradition." Following in the glorious footsteps of its previous editions, TEKMUX reiterated its story of success with its third edition.Catching up to the ever-changing dynamics of the corporate world, Startup Games became the idea-studded event of TEKMUX 3.0. Events like Infinity Hunt and Shuttersnap gave students a break to explore their flairs for adventure and photography.
